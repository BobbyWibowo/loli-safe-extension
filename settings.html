This page will help you setup your loli-safe extension so you can rehost images/audio/videos by right clicking on them. For this to work you need to provide your domain were loli-safe is running and your client token, if you chose to run it in private mode. You can also supply your admin token, so that the extension can list your albums and let you upload directly to them.
