This page will help you setup your loli-safe extension so you can rehost images/audio/videos by right clicking on them. For this to work you need to provide the domain were loli-safe is running. If you want to keep track of your uploads and be able to create albums to store them, you should create an account on the website and paste the token here, so that every upload gets tied to your account, enabling you to go back to the site and see all of your uploaded files.
